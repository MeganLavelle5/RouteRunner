What Route Runner Does

Routes built around you

Whether you're running at home or exploring a new city, Route Runner generates the perfect route in seconds.

Start Anywhere

Type any address or tap the GPS button to use your current location. Route Runner works in any city, any neighborhood, anywhere in the world.

Custom Distance

Set your exact target distance from 1 to 20 miles. Route Runner generates a route that matches your goal as closely as possible.

Terrain Preference

Choose flat roads for an easy run, mixed terrain for variety, or hilly routes for a challenge. Your run, your conditions.

Loop or Point-to-Point

Generate a loop that brings you back to your start, or set a custom destination for a point-to-point route.

Instant Route Stats

Every generated route comes with real distance, estimated time, elevation gain, and average pace so you can plan your run.

AI Route Description

Claude AI reads your route data and writes a personalized, energetic description of your run to get you motivated before you head out the door.
