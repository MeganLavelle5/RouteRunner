The Process

How It Works

From your front door to the perfect route — here's exactly how Route Runner builds your run.

Interactive Walkthrough

Click each step to explore

See exactly how Route Runner generates a personalized route just for you.

Step 1 — Enter Your Location

Start by telling Route Runner where you want to begin your run. Type any address, landmark, or neighborhood — or tap the GPS button to instantly use your current location. Route Runner works anywhere in the world.

Ways to set your start point:

  • Type a home address or street name
  • Enter a landmark (e.g. "Penn State University")
  • Use the GPS button for your current location
  • Works when traveling — any city, any country
  • Set a custom end point for point-to-point routes

Step 2 — Set Your Preferences

Tell Route Runner exactly what kind of run you're looking for. Choose your distance, whether you want a loop back to your start or a point-to-point route, and your terrain preference — flat roads, rolling hills, or a challenging hilly course.

Step 3 — AI Builds Your Route

Hit "Generate My Route" and Route Runner gets to work. The OpenRouteService API calculates a real GPS route matching your distance and terrain preferences. Then Claude AI analyzes the route and writes a personalized description to get you pumped for your run.

What happens behind the scenes:

  • Your preferences are sent to the routing engine
  • Real road & trail data is used to plan your path
  • Route is optimized for your chosen distance
  • Elevation data is calculated along the route
  • Claude AI writes your personalized route description

Step 4 — Review & Adjust

Your route appears on an interactive map in seconds. Review the key stats — actual distance, estimated time, elevation gain, and average pace. Not feeling the route? Hit "Try a Different Route" to instantly generate a fresh variation from the same start point.

Step 5 — Hit the Road!

You've got your route — now go run it! Follow the map on your phone, enjoy a fresh path you've never run before, and say goodbye to repetitive loops or getting lost in unfamiliar areas. Every run is a new adventure with Route Runner.

Why runners love Route Runner

  • Explore confidently in unfamiliar cities

  • Choose terrain that fits your training day
  • Get an AI pep talk before every run
