Who I Am

Hi! My name is Megan Lavelle.

I am a senior at Penn State studying Biomedical Engineering. Throughout the past year, I have been working to improve my mental and physical health. In doing so, I picked up outdoor running as a hobby and a form of exercise.

Outdoor running can be repetitive when following the same routes day after day or can be challenging when traveling and being unfamiliar with the roads and terrain. Route Runner is here to solve these issues!

Use Route Runner to create routes for your runs tailored to YOUR goals! Simply input your desired start and end locations, your desired distance, and your terrain preference (hilly, flat, etc), and Route Runner will generate a route designed specifically for you to help be consistent!
